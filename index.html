Frequently Asked Questions

What vaccinations are required for my pet?
We require all pets to be up to date on Rabies and Distemper/Parvo (dogs) or FVRCP (cats). Proof of vaccination is requested at your first visit.
How long does a grooming appointment take?
Most appointments take between 2-4 hours depending on your pet’s size, coat, and services requested. We will give you an estimated pick-up time when you drop off.
Do you groom cats?
Yes! We offer gentle, stress-free grooming for cats by our experienced cat specialists.
Can I stay with my pet during grooming?
For the safety and comfort of all pets, we do not allow owners in the grooming area. You are welcome to discuss any special needs or concerns before your appointment.
What if my pet is nervous or has special needs?
Please let us know in advance! We use gentle handling techniques and can make accommodations for pets with anxiety or special needs.
